# 🔥 S.A.F.E. — Smart Air & Fire Electronics

Solar-powered fire detection and GSM alert system for schools and communities.

## Features
- 🌡️ Temperature & smoke monitoring
- 🔥 IR flame detection
- 📲 GSM SMS alerts (no internet needed)
- ☀️ Solar powered with battery backup

## How It Works
Sensors monitor air and heat continuously. When fire is detected, 
a buzzer sounds and SMS alerts are sent to emergency contacts instantly.

## Components
Arduino | MQ-2 Smoke Sensor | IR Flame Sensor | SIM800L GSM | Solar Panel

## Impact
Affordable fire safety for schools and communities with no existing infrastructure.
